Background

My early years in Malabon City were characterized by a strong sense of curiosity and a desire to learn. I have always been fascinated by the wonders of technology. This fascination only grew stronger as I made my way through my formative years, inspiring me to investigate a variety of digital world aspects. During my high school years, I took a course of study which is Information and Communications Technology (ICT) to further explore the complexities of technology, driven by my natural curiosity. This educational experience prepared me for my subsequent enrollment in a college course focused on information technology (IT), where I was able to deepen my understanding of programming, website development, and application development. My sister, who also holds an IT degree, was a continual source of encouragement and support, which stoked my interest in technology even more. I have a strong affinity for all things tech, but I also have a newfound love for photography. In the fast-paced world of technology, taking pictures with a smartphone gives me a sense of peace and relaxation in addition to letting me express my creativity. My two passions, technology and photography, have helped me develop a wide range of skills and a distinct viewpoint that have shaped who I am today.
